Grand Hyatt Kochi Bolgatty

4.0   Very Good   


Located in Cochin, Grand Hyatt Kochi Bolgatty is a waterfront resort featuring two swimming pools, a tennis court, as well as a fitness centre. All rooms are air-conditioned with private bathrooms and hot tub. Rooms and suites are well equipped with workstations & flat screen TV. The rooms offer spectacular views of lake, garden, city or pool. Guests have access to the business centre, spa & salon, kids play area and some of the finest signature restaurants in the city. A continental or buffet breakfast is available daily at the property.
